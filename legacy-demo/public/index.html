How it works

From accident to action, in four beats.

The same flow whether it's a stranger on the road or a hospital triage desk.

BEAT 01

Someone scans the tag

Any phone camera works — no app, no account, no signal required to open it.

BEAT 02

Profile opens instantly

Name, blood group, conditions, allergies, and emergency contacts appear in seconds.

BEAT 03

AI briefs the responder

Gemini turns the raw profile into a 10-second spoken-style summary, flagging anything critical first.

BEAT 04

Contacts are reachable

Family and emergency numbers are one tap away, so help can be coordinated immediately.
